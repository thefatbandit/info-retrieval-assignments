For patients on seladelpar 10 milligram, the results included a nearly 80% response on our primary composite endpoint and accepted regulatory approval endpoint versus 12.5% on placebo, almost 30% response on ALP normalization versus zero on placebo and a meaningful and significant placebo-controlled effect on reducing pruritus, a key clinical symptom of PBC all in just three months.
