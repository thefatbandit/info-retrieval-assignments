For over 20 years, our current SmartBox users have recognized the benefit of using our granular insecticide products, primarily to address corn rootworm pressures which tends to be greatest in the I-70, I-80 corridor, where most farmers also grow soybeans. Adding southern soybeans and cotton acres in SIMPAS becomes a vehicle to help us increase product sales in all 3 of these major row crops throughout the United States.
