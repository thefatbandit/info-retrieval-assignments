And for Europe that means, as we are not responsible for the commercialization in Europe, we are actually not paying those royalties; we receive the royalties in the royalty range of the mid-teens to mid-twenties from our partner Incyte and, in that case, it is their business, not ours.”

MorphoSys herewith corrects the answer to this question as follows:

Under the collaboration and license agreement with Incyte Corporation, MorphoSys AG will receive tiered royalties on ex-U.S. net sales of tafasitamab in a mid-teens to mid-twenties percentage range of net sales, as previously announced on January 13, 2020. However, the related royalty payments to Xencor Inc. for product sales outside the U.S. are payable solely by MorphoSys AG and not by Incyte.
