Within the engineering and consulting segment, net revenue decreased 1.8%, which was due to the decrease in contract revenue. Thomas Brisbin

Thanks, Stacy, and good afternoon, everyone. During our call this afternoon, we will provide an update on how we are managing the company during this pandemic, highlight our second quarter activities and business development update.

Early to mid-March 2020, about 40% of our business slowed or came to a halt. To reduce costs, we had furloughs, nearly 350; layoffs, 80; reduced salaries for all indirect labor, up to 75%; reduced work hours; more fees suspended; suspension of 401k match fund; and all discretionary spending was stopped. The company took all appropriate actions to limit the effects of COVID-19’s financial impact. We also took measures to ensure the health and safety of our employees.
