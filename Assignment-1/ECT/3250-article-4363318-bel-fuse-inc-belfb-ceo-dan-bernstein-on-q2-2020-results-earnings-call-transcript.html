Many of the factors that drove this improvement are expected to provide a sustained benefit to our margins for the foreseeable future. Those factors include lower overhead cost from restructuring efforts, a reduction of material prices related to components, lower direct labor cost due to the favorable foreign exchange rates in effect during 2020 and inclusion of higher margin CUI sales.
