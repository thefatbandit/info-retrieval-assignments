Our subscription gross margin of 77% was in-line with the expectations we shared earlier in the year.

Non-GAAP operating expenses of $22.1 million were down about 14% compared to a year ago and declined as a percentage of revenue from 71% in Q2 of 2019 to 62% this year. The year-over-year improvement is primarily a result of our planned efficiencies for 2020 due to completed migrations and the additional cost management measures we implemented during Q2 and discussed on our last call.
